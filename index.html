What is the course about?

Focuses on principles of designing efficient algorithms and analyzing their performance.
Teaches evaluation of time and space complexity for algorithms.
Emphasizes understanding algorithm correctness through theoretical analysis.
Explores graph traversal techniques, sorting, searching, and advanced data structures.

How do you connect the course with real time applications?

Graph Algorithms: Use Dijkstra's or Kruskal's algorithms for route optimization in transportation networks or telecommunication systems.
Sorting and Searching: Implement efficient algorithms in e-commerce platforms for inventory management and quick product searches.
Data Structures: Apply heaps, trees, and hash tables in database indexing and real-time data retrieval systems like caching.
Optimization Problems: Solve real-world scenarios like the Knapsack problem for resource allocation in project management.

Course Project

Problem Statement : Enhancing e-commerce logistics and customer experience in a smart city focuses on leveraging advanced technologies, data analytics, and interconnected systems to streamline the flow of goods and services while ensuring a seamless, personalized shopping journey for customers

Business Cases Identified

1. Order Delivery Optimization

Optimizes delivery routes and schedules. PORTABLE TOOLS (ALGORITHMS) : Dijkstra's, Bellman-Ford , Floyd-Warshall

2. Dynamic Pricing Optimization

Adjusts prices in real-time to maximize profits. PORTABLE TOOLS (ALGORITHMS) : Quick Sort, Merge Sort, Heap Sort

3. Customer Segmentation

Groups customers to improve marketing strategies. PORTABLE TOOLS (ALGORITHMS) : Binary Search, Merge Sort, Quick Sort

4. Efficient Inventory Management

Predicts demand to maintain optimal stock levels. PORTABLE TOOLS (ALGORITHMS) : Bubble Sort, Selection Sort, Binary search.

5. Fraud Detection

Identifies fraudulent activities in transactions. PORTABLE TOOLS (ALGORITHMS) : Prim's, Kruskal's, Floyd-Warshall.

6. Product Catalog Management

Manages and organizes product listings. PORTABLE TOOLS (ALGORITHMS) : String Matching, Linear Search, Binary Search

SDG Rules

SDG 1 : No Poverty

This goal aims to eradicate extreme poverty globally, ensuring that all people have access to essential resources like food, shelter, and healthcare. It promotes equitable economic growth and social protection systems, especially for the most vulnerable populations.

SDG 4 : Quality Education

Quality education focuses on inclusive and equitable access to learning for all ages. It emphasizes lifelong learning opportunities, improved teacher training, and eliminating disparities in education, fostering personal growth and community empowerment.

SDG 5 : Gender Equality

This goal seeks to eliminate gender-based discrimination and violence, ensuring equal opportunities for all genders in education, work, and decision-making. It advocates for women's empowerment and the eradication of harmful practices like child marriage.

SDG 6 : Clean Water and Sanitation

Access to safe and affordable water and sanitation is a fundamental right. This goal addresses issues like water scarcity, pollution, and inefficient water management, ensuring that everyone has access to clean drinking water and hygienic sanitation facilities.

SDG 7 : Affordable and Clean Energy

This goal promotes access to sustainable and affordable energy for all. It encourages investment in renewable energy sources like solar, wind, and hydropower, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and mitigating climate change.

SDG 13 : Climate Action

Climate Action focuses on combating climate change by reducing greenhouse gas emissions and promoting adaptation strategies. It calls for global cooperation to implement policies that protect the environment and prepare communities for the impacts of climate change.
